This is a recipe of Pizza

-For the Pizza dough you need:

-First you need to melt the yeast in warm water

-Then add flour, sugar, oil and salt

-Then puddle it well

-Then leave it for at least 30mins

Meanwhile you can make the sauce

For the sauce you need:

-First you need chopped tomatoes

-Then you need chopped onion

-Then you need chopped garlic

-Then a small spoon of tomato sauce

-Finally a small spoon of salt and pepper

-Then you should put the garlic and the onion in the pan and grill it till it's golden

-Then add the tomatoes, the salt, the pepper and the tomato sauce

-Let it grill

-Then put it with the dough

-Then add whatever vegetables you want

-Then you finally can put it in the oven for 12-15mins

-Preparation time

.Toatal: Approximately 1 and a half hour
